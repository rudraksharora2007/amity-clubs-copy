Governance

Committee FAQs

Quick answers about the student governance and department structure at AIIT.

What is the AIIT Student Committee? ▼
It is the official governing student body of AIIT, responsible for representing student voices, coordinating inter-departmental activities, and serving as a bridge between students and faculty.
How can I become a department president? ▼
Presidents are selected annually based on their academic performance, previous leadership experience, and a formal interview process with the faculty coordinators.
What does the committee actually do? ▼
The committee manages department-specific events, handles student grievances, coordinates cultural and technical fests, and ensures smooth collaboration between all technical clubs.
Who can join the department committees? ▼
Any active student from the respective department (CS, IT, Electronics, etc.) can apply for various roles like Member Secretary, Treasurer, or Department Member.
How often does the committee meet? ▼
The full student committee meets bi-weekly with the Faculty Coordinators, while individual departments have their own weekly internal sync-ups.

General Help

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about joining and participating in AIIT Student Clubs.

What is AIIT Club and who runs it? ▼
AIIT Club is a student-led technical initiative founded and managed by the Cascade Club at AIIT, Amity University, Noida. It houses three active clubs — Cascade (Web Dev), Programming, and Networking — each operating under faculty guidance.
Do I need prior experience to join? ▼
No. All clubs are open to beginners. Each has a structured onboarding track, peer mentoring, and introductory workshops to get you started from scratch.
Is there a membership fee? ▼
Joining is completely free for all AIIT students. Some optional activities like external hackathons or third-party certifications may have a nominal fee, communicated in advance.
Can I be in multiple clubs? ▼
Yes, but we recommend focusing on one primary club in your first year for the best learning outcome. Multi-club involvement works better from second year onward.
How do I register? ▼
Fill out the Register Your Interest form on the Contact page, or walk into I-1 Block, 3rd Floor during any club's meeting hours. Registration cycles open at the start of each semester.
Are clubs faculty-run or student-run? ▼
100% student-led in terms of sessions, projects, and events. Faculty coordinators provide guidance and administrative support but don't run the day-to-day.
Will I get a certificate for participating? ▼
Yes. Participation certificates are issued for major workshops and events. Leadership roles additionally receive faculty-signed recognition letters useful for internship and placement applications.
When and where do clubs meet? ▼
At I-1 Block, 3rd Floor, Amity University UP, Sector-125, Noida. Schedules (typically weekday evenings or weekend afternoons) are shared via WhatsApp groups after you register.
Can I propose a project or event idea? ▼
Absolutely. Each club holds open planning sessions every semester where any member can pitch ideas. Strong proposals with a clear plan and team are almost always greenlit.
Is there a minimum academic requirement? ▼
No GPA cutoff for membership. Leadership roles are evaluated on club contributions, communication, and an interview — not solely academics.
